Curve Ball Bowling — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Curve Ball Bowling is developed by Chrube. This policy explains what data is handled when you play the game.

Data We Collect

The game itself does not collect, store or transmit any personal information. Your scores, records and settings are saved only on your device.

Advertising

The game shows ads served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process device information such as the advertising ID, IP address and ad interaction data. You can read how Google uses this data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads and Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Your Consent Choices

In regions where required (for example the EEA and UK), you are asked for consent before personalized ads are shown. You can change your choice at any time under Settings, using the Privacy Options button.

Children

The game does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Data Deletion

All locally stored game data is removed when you uninstall the game.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included with the next game update.
